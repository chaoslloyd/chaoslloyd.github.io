Ross Whitaker
Musician | Guitarist | Instructor

Guitar Instruction

Ross is an experienced teacher who is familiar with most contemporary styles. He takes students at all levels of age and experience. He is the guitar teacher for Lexington’s Central Music Academy, and he also runs an active private studio. If you wish to study with Ross, contact him by phone at 859-338-5787 or by email at to discuss cost and availability.

If you don't live in the Lexington area but would still like to have a lesson with Ross, he also gives lessons via Skype.

Directions to Ross’ Studio:
Ross’ studio is at 3306 Clays Mill Rd, Suite 104, which is right across the street from Jesse Clark Middle School. If you’re going out of town on Clays Mill, it will be on your left. From the entrance off of Clays Mill, 3306 is the leftmost of the two identical office buildings on the grounds. Once you enter the parking lot, you will be looking at the back (or "employee entrance") of the buildings. Go around to the other side and look for Suite 104, which is marked as Alliance Assurance. This is one of two entrance to the suite, and my studio is accessed through the other, unmarked door to the left of the marked door. I’m in room B3.
