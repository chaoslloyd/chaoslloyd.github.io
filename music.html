Ross Whitaker
Musician | Guitarist | Instructor

Music

Currently, Ross is a member of two groups: Detour Ahead, a trio that also includes Sam Flowers (trumpet, voice, piano) and Danny Cecil (bass); and Sound Foundry, a quartet with Joe Carucci (woodwinds), Danny Cecil, and Paul Deatherage (drums). If you would like to purchase Sound Foundry's 2011 self-titled album or book either of these groups for your event, contact Ross.

Ross has also been performing recently with vocalist Jesse Laine Powell and was a member of Lexington's legendary Sexual Disaster Quartet.
